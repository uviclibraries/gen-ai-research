# Workshop Activities Overview
**Pick your track** and do the activities in order. Bring a short practice paragraph and choose one tool (Perplexity, Copilot, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude).

## What you’ll learn
- Prompt basics → intermediate patterns → idea generation → critical review → privacy → pre-submit feedback → lit review helpers

## Prereqs
- One tool signed in • A practice paragraph • Willingness to redact PII

## Tracks
- **Intro**: 1-Prompt Intro → 3-Idea Generation → 4-Critical Review  
- **Intermediate**: 2-Prompt Inter → 5-Privacy → 6-Assignment Feedback  
- **Research**: 7-Lit Review

## How to use
1) Open the page, 2) copy the prompts, 3) paste your result screenshots in your notes, 4) do the self-check at the end.
